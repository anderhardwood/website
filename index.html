Our Services

REMODELING

We remodel houses, tell us everything that you want to change and after a discussion we will be able to start the job, remodeling your house to something you could've never imagined before

INSTALLATION

Installation is the process of installing the hardwood floor to a subfloor with either nails, glue, or staples.

FINISH

A finish is a top coat of water-based or oil-based stain, that gives the floor its nice every day color. Finish is an excellent method to personilze your hardwood floor.

REFINISH

Refinishing is a process of removing the top layer of the wood, so that the raw and fresh wood underneath may be revealed, so that the process of finishing may commence following this process.
